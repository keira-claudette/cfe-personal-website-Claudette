Claudette Mokeira

I was born female April 23rd 1997 and remained so these 23 years of my life.

I grew up regular,schooled in Nairobi and Nakuru interchangeably. I wouldn't say there's anything inspiring about my upbringing but it will be great to mention that I've always been ambitious. I have a natural ability to make and maintain healthy relationships with everyone around me so it's a mystery that I am not a psychiatrist. My passion for tech started out with my obsession with mobile apps.At any given point I aways have more than a hundred apps installed on my phone. At the moment I have 136 and that definitely includes Among Us!My most used apps are Google and Google keeps. I've had the priviledge to win a European Scholarship because I was incredibly good at math but the course I chose wasn't up my alley so I switched and I'll let you know I am having a great time learning web development.I'm simple,and my outlook towards life equally so. I avoid complicating things be they personal,social or work-related.I live out the law of parsimony,which in layman's states that the correct solution is the simplest.
